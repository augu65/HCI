Keystroke Biometrics

We are conduction a study to collect information on user typinging patterns. This study will have the user typing their name and a sentence 10 times. The information you provide will be able to be traced back to you due to your name being a part of the dataset. However, all data used in the report will be anonymize. The final paper will be the consolidated responses of all participants. By typing your name and clicking submit you consent to these terms. If you have any questions, please contact Jonah Stegman jstegman@uoguelph.ca.

Please only fill out this study on a computer (no phones or tablets).
